Terms & Conditions
1. Membership Agreement

By registering for GOTRE Co-Workspace, you agree to abide by our community guidelines, respect other members, and maintain a professional environment.

2. Payment Terms

Membership fees are due on the 1st of each month. A late fee may be applied for payments received after the 5th. We accept credit/debit cards and bank transfers.

3. Cancellation Policy

Members must provide at least 30 days' notice before cancellation. No refunds will be issued for unused portions of a membership period.

4. Workspace Use

Workspaces are available on a first-come, first-served basis for flexible membership plans. Dedicated desks and private offices are reserved exclusively for the registered member or team.

5. Internet Usage

Our high-speed internet is provided for professional use. We prohibit illegal downloads, streaming that consumes excessive bandwidth, and any activities that may compromise our network security.

6. Meeting Room Reservations

Meeting rooms must be booked in advance through our online portal. Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours in advance to avoid charges.

7. Guest Policy

Members may bring guests for short meetings. Guests staying longer than 2 hours must register at reception and may incur additional fees.

8. Privacy

GOTRE Co-Workspace collects personal information solely for communication and billing purposes. We will never share your information with third parties without your consent.

9. Liability

GOTRE Co-Workspace is not responsible for lost or stolen items. Members are advised to secure their belongings at all times.

10. Changes to Terms

GOTRE Co-Workspace reserves the right to modify these terms at any time. Members will be notified of significant changes via email.
